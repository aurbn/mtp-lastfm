MTP-Lastfm in an open source application that scrobbles tracks from MTP devices (such as the Creative Zen, or the Sanza Fuze) to Last.fm. You can love, ban and tag tracks before scrobbling, and also use the ratings on your device (5=Love, 1=Ban). Currently only Linux is supported.

You can check your device compatibility here. If your device is not listed, install MTP-Lastfm and mark its compatability so we can improve our database. MTP-Lastfm 0.85 and higher will automatically ask permission to do this after its first run.

MTP-Lastfm is released under the GPL and source is available at Github.
